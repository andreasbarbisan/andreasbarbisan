## Hi, I'm Andreas Barbisan

I'm an Economics undergraduate at **Insper** in Sao Paulo, Brazil, interested in applied microeconometrics, political economy, public policy, and data science. I use **R**, **Python**, **Stata**, and **LaTeX** to work with data, build reproducible analyses, and answer empirical research questions.

I completed an exchange semester at **IESEG School of Management** in Lille, France, and I plan to pursue graduate studies in Economics, with the long-term goal of building an academic career.

### Research Interests

- Applied microeconometrics
- Political economy
- Public policy evaluation
- Education, gender, and crime economics
- Data analysis and reproducible research workflows

### Selected Research Projects

- **Right-to-Carry Laws and Crime:** Panel-data analysis of right-to-carry laws and property crime in the United States.
- **Project STAR and Education Economics:** Econometric analysis of class size, teacher education, and student performance.
- **Gender Norms and Subjective Well-being:** Binary dependent variable models using World Values Survey data.
- **Women's Income and Domestic Work:** Econometric analysis of income, domestic work, education, race, and labor supply in Brazil.
